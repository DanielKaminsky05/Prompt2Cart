================================================================================
                         POTENTIAL FEATURE IDEAS
================================================================================

CORE ENHANCEMENTS
-----------------
1. Price comparison mode - Show same/similar products across different stores 
   with price differences
2. Wishlist/Save for later - Let users save items without adding to cart
3. Deal alerts - "This item is 20% off today!"
4. Bundle suggestions - "People who bought running shoes also bought these socks"

PERSONALIZATION
---------------
5. Shopping persona quiz - Quick onboarding to learn user preferences
6. Purchase history context - "Based on shirts you bought before, here are 
   matching pants"
7. Budget guardian - "You mentioned $100 budget, here's what fits"

CONVERSATIONAL FEATURES
-----------------------
8. Comparison mode - "Compare these two jackets for me"
9. Refinement prompts - "Show me cheaper options" / "What about in blue?"
10. Gift mode - "Help me find a birthday gift for my mom"
11. Occasion shopping - "I need an outfit for a wedding"

TRUST & TRANSPARENCY
--------------------
12. Sustainability score - Show eco-ratings for products
13. Store ratings - Display merchant trustworthiness
14. Return policy preview - Quick view of return terms before purchase

FUN/VIRAL FEATURES
------------------
15. Share cart - Share your curated cart with friends
16. "Surprise me" - Random product discovery based on preferences
17. Daily deal spotlight - Featured product with limited-time offer

VOICE (ELEVENLABS INTEGRATION)
------------------------------
18. Voice shopping - Speak your query instead of typing
19. Voice product descriptions - Hear product details read aloud
20. Voice checkout confirmation - Audio confirmation of order
